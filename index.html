Dr. Amulya Bhattarai

Chairperson of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Assumption University, Bangkok, Thailand

Director of Smart and Sustainable Engineering Solutions, Kathmandu/Pokhara, Nepal

About Me

Responsible and efficient manager who oversees the resource allocation of subjects, research among lecturers and students. Performs the proper documentation and accreditation by COE, OHEC along with coordinates harmoniously with various departments/offices.

Decades of experience in teaching basic foundational subjects: Calculus, Physics, Electronics to advanced and trending ECE topics: Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Communication (Wireless, Antenna, Digital), Control Systems, Smart Grid with handon research, project based teaching and learning with dozens of publications in international conferences and journals (IEEE, SCOPUS, SPRINGER, ACM).

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI), IoT, Machine Learning (RL, NN) Initiatives
  • Electronics (Communication) Systems: Smart Antennas, Cognitive Radios, SDR
  • Renewable (Solar, Hydro, Geothermal) Energy and Smart Grid Systems
