Dr. Amulya Bhattarai

Chairperson of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Assumption University, Bangkok, Thailand

Director of Smart and Sustainable Engineering Solutions, Kathmandu/Pokhara, Nepal

About Me

Optimal and efficient resource allocation: subjects, research among lecturers and students. Documentation on accreditation by COE, OHEC. Coordination with various departments/offices.

Decades of experience in teaching basic Science subjects (Calculus, Physics, Electronics) to advanced ECE topics (Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Communication (Wireless, Antenna, Digital), Control Systems, Smart Grid) with handon research and dozens of publications in international conferences and journals (IEEE, SCOPUS, SPRINGER, ACM).

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI), IoT, Machine Learning (RL, NN) Initiatives
  • Electronics (Communication) Systems: Smart Antennas, Cognitive Radios, SDR
  • Renewable (Solar, Hydro, Geothermal) Energy and Smart Grid Systems
